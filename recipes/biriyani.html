Biriyani Recipe

This is the famous biriyani of Hyderabad. This biriyani is loved by every Indian.

Ingredients needed for the recipe:

  • 2 cups Basmati Rice (soaked for 30 mins)
  • 500g Chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 cup Yogurt (Dahi)
  • 2 Onions (sliced and fried till golden)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Biriyani Masala or Garam Masala
  • Saffron soaked in warm milk
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Salt and oil/ghee
  • The most important ingredient: PATIENCE

Steps to prepare Hyderabadi Chicken Biriyani:

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, masala, salt, and a bit of lemon juice. Let it rest for at least 1 hour.
  2. Cook the rice in boiling water till itâ€™s 70% done. Drain and keep aside.
  3. In a deep pot, heat some oil/ghee. Add the marinated chicken and layer half of the fried onions, mint, and coriander.
  4. Spread the semi-cooked rice on top. Sprinkle the remaining onions, saffron milk, mint, coriander, and a little ghee.
  5. Seal the lid (with dough if you want it authentic) and cook on low flame (dum) for 25-30 minutes.
  6. Turn off heat and let it rest for 10 minutes. Then gently mix and serve hot!
