Shawarma Recipe

This is the famous Shawarma of Mumbai. The Shawarma franchise name is Pamper Belly. Most of the crowd from Mumbai comes here to eat this juicy Shawarma.

Ingredients needed for the recipe:

  • 4 Tortilla wraps or Khabus
  • 500g boneless Chicken (cut into thin strips)
  • 1 tbsp Garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Paprika or Kashmiri Red Chili Powder
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Shredded Lettuce
  • Sliced Onions and Tomatoes
  • Mayonnaise or Garlic Sauce
  • Chipotle or Peri Peri Sauce (optional)
  • Pickles (optional)

Steps to prepare Shawarma:

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, garlic paste, lemon juice, paprika, salt, and pepper. Let it rest for at least 1 hour (overnight is better).
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© the marinated chicken until fully cooked and slightly crispy.
  3. Warm the tortilla wraps or khabus slightly on a tawa or pan.
  4. Place lettuce, cooked chicken, onions, and tomatoes on the wrap.
  5. Add garlic sauce, mayo, and optional sauces like chipotle or peri peri.
  6. Roll the wrap tightly like a burrito and toast lightly on a pan (optional).
  7. Cut in half, serve hot with fries or coleslaw!
