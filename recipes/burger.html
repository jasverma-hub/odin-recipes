Burger Recipe

This is the Chicken Whopper burger from Burger King.

Ingredients needed for the recipe:

  • 2 Burger buns
  • 1 Chicken patty (grilled or fried)
  • 1 Cheese slice
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion rings
  • Tandoori mayonnaise or regular mayo
  • Butter (for toasting buns)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps to prepare Chicken Burger:

  1. Toast the burger buns lightly with some butter on a pan or tawa.
  2. Grill or fry the chicken patty until golden brown and cooked through.
  3. Spread tandoori mayo on the inside of both bun halves.
  4. Place lettuce, tomato, and onion on the bottom bun.
  5. Add the chicken patty and top with a cheese slice.
  6. Cover with the top bun and press gently.
  7. Serve hot with fries and a cold drink!
